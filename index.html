My First Post - Velkommen!

Welcome to the personal blog of Nate Wagar, frontend developer & architect, traveler, and armchair philosopher.

I’m writing this first post from the Oslo Airport, where my new wife and I are winding down our honeymoon. I hope to soon collect my musings and Danielle’s photographs on our visit to Iceland and Norway in a post of their own, but for now let me just say that I love this country and its culture of being unphased by mild cold. I am, however, ready to get home to whatever crazy November weather awaits us in Rochester.

I am, among other things, a Software Architect for Paychex, a student of Roberts Wesleyan College, a fairweather fan of the Packers, Yankees, and Everton (and an all-weather fan of the Bills through marriage), an entrepreneur with Phrankly, and a guy that ends every summer with the phrase “I should have hiked more”.

I don’t know what to tell you to expect of my writings here, but they will almost certainly consist of ramblings on a topic from work, armchair critiques of some current event, or musings on my latest quarterly hobby.
